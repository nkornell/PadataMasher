Steps

  1. Indicate how your datafile is organized.
  2. Drag and drop your datafile onto the program. Your users' responses will appear.
  3. Click on the correct responses. Those responses will get a score of 1 in the datafile.
  4. When you are done, press Export Results to Desktop.
Autoscore
Autoscore will select correct answers for you, including misspelled answers. It isn't always perfect, so double check it. (Autoscore ignores columns where accuracy is perfect, or very low, and any column name that starts with the word "Timing."

Privacy
This program keeps your data private. Nothing is ever uploaded.
